KEY CALCULATIONS
???????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????

Productivity Score Formula:
  Score = (TaskCompletionRate × 50%) + (PerformanceRating/5 × 100 × 50%)
  Range: 0-100

Task Completion Rate:
  Rate = (TasksCompleted / (TasksCompleted + TasksPending)) × 100
  Range: 0-100%

Performance Status:
  5 Star ? "Excellent"
  4 Star ? "Good"
  3 Star ? "Average"
  2 Star ? "Below Average"
  1 Star ? "Poor"

TROUBLESHOOTING TIPS
???????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????

If charts don't appear:
? Ensure Chart.js CDN is loaded (already in view)
? Check browser console for JavaScript errors
? Verify data is being passed to View

? Refresh the page
? Check database has the record
? Verify employee is assigned to a department

? Verify the migration was applied
? Check the database columns exist
? Review the formula in CalculateProductivityScore()
